January 2, 2017

Alexa: Can you tell me about yourself?

Name: Uhhh I’ve been working for the past ummm six years as a systems analyst and data manager. During that time I’ve been trained and certified on like a number of different software platforms and systems.

Alexa: What do you consider to be your weakness?

Name:I get really umm nervous speaking in front of other people. Normally this isn’t really like a problem but I’ve found that there have been times during meetings when I’ve kept quiet and not shared my ideas or insights. So um, as a result things that should have been brought up and problems that should have been addressed early on weren’t, meaning more work for me as I had to deal with them as they arose.

Alexa: Tell me about a challenge you've faced at work and how you dealt with it.

Name:My manager and I had a miscommunication about a project for a new client. I had asked for a deadline extension, and he approved it. However, closer to the deadline, he didn’t remember our agreement and became very angry that parts of the project weren’t ready. I briefly explained my position and then suggested a plan to provide the client with our finished work and follow up quickly with the rest. My manager and I later agreed to start formalizing important changes in writing to avoid confusion.
